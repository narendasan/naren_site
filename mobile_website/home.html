Prospective Student Events

The following events are available for prospective 9th graders:

  • Showcase Night: November 28th
  • Breakfast with the Principal: Parents will have an opportunity to meet with our Principal, Don Stensrud, and ask questions about Fairview. Please contact Jenny McCarthy at 720-561-5329 to make a reservation.
  • Fairview Experience: This event is designed for incoming freshmen to experience Fairview. Click here to make a reservation.
  • Parent Tours: Parent Tours take place during the Fairview Experience and are conducted by an Administrator. Come see Fairview and get your questions answered. No reservation required.

Christmas Tree Decoration Drive

Our Fairview club Fashion for A Cause is working with the local organization There With Care to collect Christmas tree decorations (ornaments, lights, tree skirts). A total of 80 trees were donated to families that have children with critical illnesses, but they have no decorations. New and used decorations will be collected through November 30th. There will be a bin in the vestibule inside the main front door for donations to be dropped off. Your help is greatly appreciated!
